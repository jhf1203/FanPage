Put Together Beautifully, a Tribute To The National

The National is an American rock band from Cincinnati, Ohio, formed in 1999. Four of the band's albums were included on NME's 2013 list of the 500 greatest albums of all time

Band Members

  • Matt Berninger (vocals)
  • Aaron Dessner (guitar, piano, keyboards, harmonica, mandola)
  • Bryce Dessner (guitar, piano, keyboards)
  • Scott Devendorf (bass)
  • Bryan Devendorf (drums)

Style

literate rock that presents at first as artily sombre, and eventually as one of the most nuanced 21st-century iterations of what used to be known as “college rock”.

Kitty Empire, The Guardian

The National has been compared to Joy Division, Leonard Cohen, Interpol, Wilco, Depeche Mode and Nick Cave & the Bad Seeds. The band's lyrics have been described as "dark, melancholy and difficult to interpret." They have released 8 studio albums in the last 20 years

Personal

The National has followed me through adulthood, and I always find relevance to my own life in Matt's lyrics and an atmosphere from the band's instrumentation that can serve as a soundtrack for my life in any situation. From my first listen of "Mr. November" in college to relaxing with a glass of whiskey last night with "Dark Side Of the Gym" in my headphones, The National has been there with me throughout my 20s and 30s and will remain a staple that I hope to share with my son in the future. With that I will leave you with my favorite National track.
